Privacy Policy for Cqmbo's Bot

Last Updated: April 2026

This policy explains how Cqmbo's Bot ("the Bot") handles your data. My goal is to keep the server safe from automated spam and ban evasion while respecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

To verify your identity and prevent "alt" accounts from bypassing server rules, the Bot processes the following information when you click a verification link:
Discord User ID: To link your verification status to your account.
IP Address: Processed temporarily to check for VPNs and proxies.

2. How We Use Your Data

VPN Detection: Your IP address is sent to a third-party API (IPHub) to determine if you are using a virtual private network or proxy.
Alt Detection: We use a cryptographic "hash" of your IP address. This turns your IP into a long string of random letters and numbers (e.g., a8f5f...).
We do not store your raw IP address. We only store the hash. This allows us to see if two Discord accounts are joining from the same connection without us actually knowing what your IP address is.

3. Data Retention

Verification Logs: Records of successful or failed verifications are stored in our private database to maintain your "Member" status.
Data Deletion: If you leave the server and wish for your hashed data to be removed, please contact a Server Administrator.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare to secure our connection and IPHub to verify network integrity. These services may process your IP address according to their own privacy policies to provide these security features.

5. Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact the Server Manager via Discord.
